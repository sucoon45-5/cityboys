National DG

Hon. Shoga Francis Oluwatosin — A Visionary Leader, Procurement Expert, and Catalyst for Youth Engagement.

The Engine of Renewed Hope

Strategic Leadership

Built on Academic Excellence

Francis Oluwatosin Shoga was born on July 7, 1979. His journey to the pinnacle of leadership began with a solid educational foundation, starting at Loyola College, Ibadan. He proceeded to obtain a Bachelor of Science in Banking and Finance from Olabisi Onabanjo University.

Driven by a passion for continuous growth, he earned an MBA from the National Open University of Nigeria and is currently completing an M.Sc. in Banking and Finance at Baze University, Abuja. This academic rigor forms the bedrock of his strategic approach to management and advocacy.

Professional Mastery

16 Years of Public Excellence

Technical Adviser

Served in the Office of the Vice President on Public Procurement, overseeing multi-billion naira contracts and national social investment programs.

Integrity & Transparency

Ensured total compliance with the Public Procurement Act 2007, guaranteeing transparency across critical sectors like roads, agriculture, and defense.

The Catalyst

Gallero Global & The Movement

After 16 years of exemplary public service, Francis voluntarily bowed out to pursue his passion for entrepreneurship. As Managing Director of Gallero Global Resources, he delivers innovative solutions in logistics and procurement consulting.

Today, he serves as the Director General of City Boy Movement, a foremost sociopolitical organization. Under his stewardship, the movement has become a powerful platform for advocating the President’s policies and mobilizing grassroots support across the federation.
