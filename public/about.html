Our Pulse

Who We Are & What We Stand For

City Boy Movement is built on the belief that a better society is achieved when citizens stand together with a shared vision. We are more than a political organization; we are a community-driven initiative dedicated to protecting civic rights and empowering every voice.

Youth-Led Governance Advocacy

Grassroots Social Empowerment

Our movement bridges the gap between the grassroots and policy-making levels, ensuring that sustainable opportunities and economic growth are accessible to all Nigerians, particularly the next generation.

Our Vision

Build a united and empowered youth population driving national development.

We envision a Nigeria where every young citizen is informed, active, and a catalyst for political stability and economic transparency.

Our Mission

  • Empower youth through practical skill acquisition.
  • Facilitate civic education and awareness.
  • Champion grassroots community development.

We are dedicated to building a progressive society where voice matters and opportunity is accessible to all.

Core Values

Values in Motion

The principles that guide every action and initiative we take across Nigeria.

Human Dignity

Ensuring every citizen is respected, protected, and given a genuine chance to thrive through fairness and equal rights.

Social Growth

Promoting long-term solutions that support economic stability and the responsible use of national resources.

Active Participation

Encouraging active involvement in governance and leadership development at all levels of society.
